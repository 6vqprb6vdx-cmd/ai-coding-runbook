Project visibility and sharing features are available to users on Team or Enterprise plans. This article assumes that public projects are enabled for your organization. For more information, see How can I disable public projects?

When creating a project on a Team or Enterprise plan, you can choose between two initial visibility options:

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/lupk8zyo/1740370991/2b6b16e5deff094e073a5b4bb0ea/63197103-24c0-41e5-aebd-9b8f431837bb?expires=1777916700&amp;signature=d2f077e7e3901127b20ebdefd8f10de1f7e9fad0ecae14402dd56fa1d5b17a16&amp;req=dScjFsp5nYhWWPMW1HO4zd3a1lstJYigHK95%2FTFaPym8Mixq2zrmbV7QLQLY%0AwIm9Mk7B3RM4efT0tp0%3D%0A)

If you choose to share a project with the rest of your organization upon creation, this means that any other member of your team can find it by browsing or searching the Team tab in the Projects section, and use it to start a chat. Even if a project is public, your chats within that project will be private and inaccessible to other members of your organization unless you manually share them.
