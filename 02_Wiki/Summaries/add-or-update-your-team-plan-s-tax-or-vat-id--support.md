When you sign up for a Team plan, you may have the option to enter your Tax or VAT ID depending on your location:

Important: If you're signing up as a business and want to use a name other than the one tied to your payment method, an organization Owner should check the "Use a different name on invoices" box when adding or updating your payment method in Organization settings > Billing. Then, enter your company name in the "Bill to" field to ensure it is reflected correctly on your invoice.

Covers: Add your tax or VAT ID during signup; Update your tax or VAT ID after signup.
