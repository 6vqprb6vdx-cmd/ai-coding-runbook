Key items: Write across different formats and styles. Claude can help you draft everything from a text message or an email to a screenplay or a novel.; Learn something new. Claude is great at generating concise summaries of long articles, news stories, documents, conversations, or even books!; Code. Claude can be an extremely helpful 'pair programmer'.
