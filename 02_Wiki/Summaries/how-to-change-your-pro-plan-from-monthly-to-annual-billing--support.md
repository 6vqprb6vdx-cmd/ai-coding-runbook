Save on your Pro plan subscription by switching from monthly to annual billing. When you upgrade, any remaining time on your current monthly subscription will be credited to your new annual plan invoice.

Covers: To switch from monthly to annual billing; What happens next?. Key items: Navigate to Settings > Billing.; Click "Adjust plan" next to your Pro subscription details.; From the upgrade page, select "Get Pro annual plan"; Review the annual price on the next screen..
