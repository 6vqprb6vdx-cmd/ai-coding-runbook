This article explains how Claude for Government Owners and Primary Owners can contact Anthropic Support. We've designed a specialized process to ensure your sensitive information remains secure while providing you with the help you need.

Covers: How to Get Started. Key items: Log in to your Owner or Primary Owner Claude for Government account.; Click your initials or name in the lower left corner and select “Get help” from the menu.; You will be directed to a page describing additional guidelines for our specialized support process.; Follow the guidelines described on that page to submit a support inquiry..
