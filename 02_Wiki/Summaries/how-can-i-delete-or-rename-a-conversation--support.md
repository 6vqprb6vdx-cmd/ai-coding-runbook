Covers: Deleting and renaming individual conversations; Deleting conversations in bulk. Key items: Navigate to the conversation you want to delete or rename.; Click on the name of the conversation at the top of the screen.; Select either "Delete" or "Rename" from the options that appear:; Navigate to your chat history by clicking "Chats" on the left sidebar..
