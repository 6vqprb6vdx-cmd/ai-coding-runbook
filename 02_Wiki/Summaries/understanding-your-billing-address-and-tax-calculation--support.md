Your billing address determines how taxes are calculated on your Claude purchases. This address should represent the primary place of business receiving the product.

Your billing address is automatically set to match your payment method's billing address. To update it, navigate to Settings > Billing and click “Update” to the right of your current payment method.

You can update your billing address directly in Settings → Billing → Billing Addresses. Changes take effect immediately for future invoices.

Your billing address is tied to your payment method. Update your payment method by navigating to Settings > Billing in the Claude Console and clicking the pencil icon to the right of your current card number to change your billing address.

Covers: How billing addresses work by plan type; Pro, Max, and self-serve Team plans; Sales-assisted Team plans and Enterprise plans; API (usage tier 1-4 / self-serve); Paid API contracts; Requesting a different billing address.
