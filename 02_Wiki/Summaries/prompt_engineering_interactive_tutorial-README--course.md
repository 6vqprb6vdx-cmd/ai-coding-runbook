Comprehensive step-by-step interactive tutorial on engineering optimal prompts within Claude.

**Outcomes.** Master the basic structure of a good prompt; recognize common failure modes and the 80/20 techniques to address them; understand Claude's strengths and weaknesses; build strong prompts from scratch for common use cases.

**Structure.** 9 chapters with accompanying exercises plus an appendix of advanced methods. Work through chapters in order. Each lesson has an "Example Playground" area for live experimentation, plus a Google Sheets answer key.

Note: tutorial defaults to Claude 3 Haiku (cheapest/fastest); Sonnet and Opus are more intelligent (Opus the most). A Google Sheets / Claude for Sheets version is also available and recommended for user-friendliness.

**Table of contents:**

- **Beginner** — Chapter 1: Basic Prompt Structure; Chapter 2: Being Clear and Direct; Chapter 3: Assigning Roles.
- **Intermediate** — Chapter 4: Separating Data from Instructions; Chapter 5: Formatting Output & Speaking for Claude; Chapter 6: Precognition (Thinking Step by Step); Chapter 7: Using Examples.
- **Advanced** — Chapter 8: Avoiding Hallucinations; Chapter 9: Building Complex Prompts (Industry Use Cases) including Chatbot, Legal Services, Financial Services exercise, Coding exercise, Congratulations & Next Steps.
- **Appendix: Beyond Standard Prompting** — Chaining Prompts, Tool Use, Search & Retrieval.
