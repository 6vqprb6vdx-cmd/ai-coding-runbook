The features described in this guide are available on iOS 18 and above.

Quickly access Claude app functionality from your Lock Screen, Control Center, or Action Button on iOS. This article will explain how to add the Claude app into these areas.

You’ll now be able to tap and hold to open the Claude app from your Lock Screen.

You’ll now be able to access the Claude app from your Control Center.

You’ll now be able to Hold to Open the Action Button to access the Claude app.

Covers: **Add Claude app to your Lock Screen**; **Add Claude app to Control Center**; **Use the Action Button to Open Claude app**.
