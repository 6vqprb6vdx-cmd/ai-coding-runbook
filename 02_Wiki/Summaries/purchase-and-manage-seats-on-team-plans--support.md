Seat management allows Team plan owners to control their organization's seat allocation, assign users to different seat types, and manage billing. For pricing and billing details, see How is my Team plan bill calculated?

Permissions note: Only Owners and Primary Owners can purchase seats and access Organization settings > Billing. Admins and above can reassign seat types for members in Organizationsettings > Organization.

For information on adding and removing members from your organization, see Managing members on Team and Enterprise plans.

| Seat type | What's included | | --- | --- | | Standard | Base features, usage limits, and Claude Code access | | Premium | Everything in Standard, plus higher usage limits |

Covers: Understanding seat types; Purchasing new seats; Reducing your seat allocation; Assigning and reassigning seat types; Upgrading a Standard seat to Premium; What if I don't have an available seat?; Using "unassigned" to swap users between seat types; Seat assignment with JIT or SCIM provisioning.
