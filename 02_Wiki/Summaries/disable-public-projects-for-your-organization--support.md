This feature is available to Primary Owners and Owners on Team and Enterprise plans.

Disabling the public project feature allows Team and Enterprise owners to restrict the creation of public projects across their organization while maintaining internal sharing capabilities.

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/lupk8zyo/2053902291/8c39d1a79dedc97411eed54dec5c/CleanShot+2026-02-11+at+11_25_34%402x.png?expires=1777916700&amp;signature=874ee411411b78b6bc13bd8da8209f93fdf76d726e435227816e13929bc5b91b&amp;req=diAiFcB%2Bn4NWWPMW1HO4zfGiYGCtagZYYabJlVJ9VPzWRMkYb%2FfXsa3DTJFI%0AjnvIn4K2NuyA6CPMrog%3D%0A)

By default, public projects are enabled for all Team and Enterprise plans. When you disable public projects:

Important: Users can still share private projects with individual users within your organization after disabling public projects. Any private projects that were shared with individuals will retain their shared settings.

Disabling public projects does not affect the internal project sharing capabilities available to Team and Enterprise users:

Covers: How does disabling public projects work?; Impact on project sharing; Re-enable public projects.
