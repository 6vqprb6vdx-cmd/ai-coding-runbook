Owners and Primary Owners of Team plans with monthly subscriptions can switch from the monthly plan to an annual plan by following these steps:

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/lupk8zyo/1690326039/3a91cdc5fff57d188a18ecc6273f/image.png?expires=1778004000&amp;signature=d5049c463264956b39f036a0b8534fe1678937d7a73f6771f22e3ecea114aace&amp;req=dSYuFsp8m4FcUPMW3Hu4gbNj%2Bk7zVA3hj%2B5vzcg6znWuTpHmXUHq3tUyPmhB%0AnQ%3D%3D%0A) Key items: Navigate to Organization settings > Billing or claude.ai/upgrade.; You’ll see a banner confirming your current monthly plan in your Billing settings; click the “Switch to Annual” button.; Or from /upgrade, click the “Switch to Annual plan” button:; The confirmation screen will display the total cost for your upgrade from monthly to annual billing:.
