Anthropic PBC discloses account records solely in accordance with our Terms of Service and applicable law. For more information about Anthropic’s policies for handling governmental requests for user information, see here. If you are a law enforcement agent who is authorized to gather evidence in connection with an official investigation, you may request records from Anthropic by emailing <law-enforcement-requests@anthropic.com>. Key items: Law Enforcement Agency Country; City; State/Province; Zip/Postal Code.
