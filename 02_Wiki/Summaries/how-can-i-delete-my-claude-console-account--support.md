Only Console Admins can request deletion. See Claude Console Roles and Permissions for a full overview of your role's capabilities.

This article explains how a Claude Console Admin can delete their organization via Console settings. Note that deletion impacts all members of a Console organization.

If you followed the steps above to delete your Console organization but want to revert this action, our Support team can restore your account data within seven days of deletion. Please contact Support for further assistance.

If you have an outstanding balance, you will see a message during the deletion flow that prompts you to pay the balance first by routing you to Settings > Billing.
