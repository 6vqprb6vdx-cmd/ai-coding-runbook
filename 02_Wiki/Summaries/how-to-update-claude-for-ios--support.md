If you aren’t seeing the latest features on Claude for iOS, you may need to update your mobile app manually by following these instructions from Apple: Key items: Open the App Store.; Tap the "My Account" button or your photo at the top of the screen.; Scroll down to see pending updates.; Tap "Update" next to Claude for iOS to update it to the newest version..
