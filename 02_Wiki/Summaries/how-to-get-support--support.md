This guide explains how to get support for your Claude or Console account. Different support options are available depending on your plan.

Note: While we don't offer phone or live chat support, our Product Support team will gladly assist you through our support messenger.

Users who are logged in to their Claude or Console accounts will be able to click the icon in the lower right corner of any Help Center page to open the support messenger and start chatting with Fin.

Important: For Console users, this applies specifically to those logged in via platform.claude.com. If you’re logged in via console.anthropic.com, you will be prompted to visit platform.claude.com to authenticate before sending a message in the Help Center support messenger.

Covers: Pro and Max plans, Team and Enterprise plan Owners, and Console Admins; Seeking support for Claude; Seeking support for the Claude API or Console; Seeking support for any account type from the Help Center; Team and Enterprise plan non-Owners, and Console non-Admins; Enterprise plan Owners; Free Claude users; Unable to log in?.
